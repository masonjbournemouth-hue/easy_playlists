

PRIVACY POLICY FOR PLAYLIST CITY

Playlist City respects your privacy. This app is designed to let you create and enjoy playlists from the music stored on your own device.


INFORMATION WE COLLECT

No personal data is collected. The app does not require you to log in, create an account, or provide personal information. Playlist City only accesses audio files already stored on your device to create and play playlists.


DATA STORAGE

No information is transmitted to external servers. All data (such as your playlists) is stored locally on your device only.


THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Playlist City does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.


CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This app is safe for all ages as it does not collect or share any personal data.
